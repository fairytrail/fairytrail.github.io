Fairytrail

How It Works


 
We welcome individuals from all nationalities to sign up for Fairytrail, however all of users are expected to:
 
Be a single and over the age of 18. Have working English proficiency. Afford paying for own trips. Be an employee or work for themselves.
 


 
1. Find your partner.
 
Create your profile and set your preferences. We will look for your ideal partners based on those preferences from around the world. Everytime you search, we’ll bring you new profiles to like or pass.
 
If you like someone who also likes you, you’ve matched!
 

 

2. Match on a place you’d like to explore.
 
Next, you’ll see 10 adventures you can go on with that person. Choose up to 3 for free. Your match will also see the same 10 adventures to choose from.
 
Fairytrail is full of serendipity! If you match on any of the adventures, you’ll be approved to book it. If not, you'll be unmatched forever.
 

 

3. Book the trip and start getting to know each other!
 
Pay the $15 refundable deposit to show you’re sincere about joining the group adventure.
 
Once both parties have made the deposit, you'll be connected. Get to know each other and complete the booking together within 30 days.
 
The adventure is provided by Airbnb or TripAdvisor! When you book together, we buy two seats on the adventure, so you'll be with the general public. Not a bunch of other people on first dates. This creates a social, low-stress environment for you to have fun while getting to know your partner.
 
Happy exploring together!
 

 

One last note.
 
After each trip, you will be asked rate the adventure and your partner to keep our adventures incredible and our community safe.
