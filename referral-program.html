Our Referral Programs


 

Free Adventures Program
 
The "Free Adventures" referral program is available to all Fairytrail users with an account status of approved.
 
You will earn $10 USD in travel money for each new user who enters your referral code and then gets a match.
 
The amount you can earn in travel money is capped at $500 USD. Your referred user will not get any compensation for signing up as per Apple's new policy.


 

 

 

Ambassador Program
 
The "Ambassador" referral program is an invite-only program for select Fairytrail users. To request consideration, you may contact us at team@fairytrail.app with a self-introduction and reasons why you'd be a good ambassador/affiliate.
 
When your Fairytrail account is approved for the Ambassador program, you will earn $1 for each new user who enters your referral code and gets a match. Your earnings will be uncapped and you can choose to be paid out in either travel money or cash.
 
The referred user gets $10 USD in travel money for using your code.


 

 

Abuse and Fraud
 
These programs are intended to reward users who help Fairytrail grow its user base. If you are found to be creating fake accounts in order to abuse our programs or are engaged in any fraudulent behavior, we may ban or even delete your account. Your earned rewards will not be paid out in such cases.
 
These terms are subject to change anytime at the sole discretion of Fairytrail.
