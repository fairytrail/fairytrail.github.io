8 Levels of Safety

We take two things seriously here at Fairytrail: fun and safety. In fact, our travel and dating safety standards extend from online to offline.
 
First, our safety team manually screens all new users using our world-class approval system that works in the background.
 
Second, we require all users to verify themselves via a small entrance fee. This is one of the attributes that feed into our fraud detection alogrithm.
 
Third, we allow users to block, report, and rate other users to keep the community safe and kind. This feedback is used by our safety algorithm but not publicly displayed.
 
Fourth, we offer profile verification through Checkr, the service that powers Uber and Lyft.
 
Fifth, we require users to video chat to determine chemistry and comfort before we allow them to book through the trip through our travel agency.
 
Sixth, our adventures are in groups of 5-25 people (includes non-Fairytrail members) and led by an experienced guide. No need to stay in the same place or engage in any activities outside the adventure.
 
Seventh, our adventures are operated by reputable companies including Airbnb and Viator. We leverage their services because they are high-quality, plentiful, and safe.
 
For non-911 related emergencies, we have a safety response line for when you may wish to speak to someone if you do not feel safe.
