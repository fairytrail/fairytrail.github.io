✈️ Booking the Adventure
 
When you contact us to book the adventure, we wait until both of you have paid before processing it, so no one is on the hook.
 
Once the booking is confirmed, you may not cancel or modify your booking anymore. We recommend you purchase travel insurance if you are unsure or unable to fully commit.
 
The final adventure price may vary and includes only the activities although some packages may include food, accommodations, and transport. Our agents can help you book flights and hotels for no extra service fee. Just ask.
