Making a Reservation
 
Pay a $15 refundable deposit to show you're sincere about joining the adventure.
 
This is a free service we provide to increase your chances of meeting in person and developing a relationship by keeping time-wasters and scammers out of our community.
 
Once you and your match have paid deposits, you'll be connected. Message and video chat with each other to see if there's chemistry. Complete the booking together within 30 days if things go well.
 
Satisfaction guaranteed. If you want your deposit back, contact us to receive a full refund.
 

 

Booking the Adventure
 
When you contact us to book the adventure, we wait until both of you have paid before processing it, so no one is on the hook. Your deposit is automatically applied as a credit.
 
Once the booking is confirmed, you may not cancel or modify your booking anymore. We recommend you purchase travel insurance if you are unsure or unable to fully commit.
 
The final tour price may vary and includes only the activities although some packages may include food, accommodations, and transportation. Our agents can help you book flights and hotels for no extra service fee. Just ask.
